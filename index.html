Project 1: Flarf

For this project, students were to practice creating a website with a flarf poem. Although it can be described
as 'incoherrent', a Flarf poem is by taking a phrase and searching it up on the internet. You take the first couple website
along with image results and use those to create a poem. You are allowed to change some
things up to make your best work. My words were 'Girl, I think I messed up' which created my work
Love-ache.




Project 2: Green Screen

For this project, we were to make a background (specifically an animated or video-type one) which could be
used as a zoom background. I decided to try out puppet animation in AE with Hatsune Miku.
This video shows the final result.






Project 3: Twine

For this project, we were to create a non-linear twine game. For my game, it was one of coming
to terms with grief and sorrow. While short, it was created in a way where players are allowed to choose to deal
with grief's stages in the way they want to. This also reflects how different people in real life deal with grief differently.

How will you deal with your grief?
